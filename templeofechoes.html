The Mirror of Ages

The Temple of Echoes rises as a cathedral of wind and mirrors, its jagged spires clawing the sky, whispering lost ages. Liora guards its secrets, guiding Aria through puzzles—Wind Turbine Lock, Light Prism Array, Cyclone Dial Conundrum. Raptors dive from above, their screeches echoing as Aria’s resolve meets the temple’s trials, her fiery hair a beacon against the wind-swept shadows.
