Behold, warriors of fate, the genesis of a legend! For thirty moons—over two relentless years—the Valley of the Ancients has clawed its way from whispers to a roaring crucible of destiny. Beneath the Teeth of the Sky, I’ve stood with you, plotting every jagged peak, every emerald snarl, every rift’s festering wound. Aria, our Last Guardian, rises from her mother Lysara’s blood-soaked sacrifice, her silver hair a banner against Umbraeth’s creeping shroud. Below, a glimpse of our blueprint—a map of scars and secrets, etched in shadow and gold. This is no mere game; it’s a saga born of our shared fire. Step into the forge with me—the echoes awaken now!
