Ryn

Sharp-eyed scout of the jungle’s thorns

The Jungle Scout

Profile

Name: Ryn

Role: Ally, Scout of the Living Jungle

Gender: Male

Age: 32

Appearance

Wiry and lean—tanned skin streaked with jungle dirt, black ragged hair matted with sap, a gray streak from a raptor’s graze. Gray eyes glint like flint, clad in a leather-vine jerkin patched with feathers, boots silent on stone.

Personality

Sharp and pragmatic—Ryn’s wit cuts quick, his humor a survivor’s shield over a guarded heart. Cynical yet loyal when earned, his growl softens with camaraderie, testing Aria’s mettle with jibes.

Abilities

Health: 80 | Stamina: 60 | Essence: 10 | Agility: 12 | Resistance: 5

Dagger Slash: Precise strikes (8 damage, 4 Stamina)—nimble and fierce.

Scout’s Throw: Dagger hurl (10 damage, 8 Stamina)—targets weak points.

Herb Patch: Heals (+10 Health)—rough aid from his Scout’s Pouch.

Background

Born in the Living Jungle, Ryn lost his father to a golem at 12, inheriting a Vinewood Dagger and a survivor’s creed. He’s roamed since, dodging claws and thorns—Aria’s fiery hair sparks a purpose beyond scavenging at the Spire.

Role

Ryn aids Aria’s early trials—his jungle savvy and quips lighten her burden, a cynical mirror whose loyalty grows, guiding her through the wild with a scout’s sharp eye.
