The Cliffside Haven

The Tribal Enclave clings to jagged cliffs, huts trembling under wind and shadow’s choice. Mirra and Dren plead for Aria’s aid against Kael’s reign—her decision (Stormforged Spear or Warlord’s Mark) shapes its fate. Casters weave curses as gruntlings swarm, but victory rebuilds the Enclave, its fires steady against the fading dark, a testament to Aria’s fiery resolve.
