Liora

Hermit of the Temple’s wind-worn song

The Temple Hermit

Profile

Name: Liora

Role: Ally, Guardian of the Temple of Echoes

Gender: Female

Age: 52

Appearance

Stooped yet sturdy—gnarled hands and weathered skin beneath a moss-patched cloak. Gray, tangled hair woven with wind chimes clinks softly, ice-blue eyes pierce like frost. Vinewood boots tread silently, a hermit melded with the Temple’s ruins.

Personality

Wise and stern—Liora’s raspy voice carries solitude’s weight, softened by compassion for Aria. Gruff yet nurturing, her sternness shields a heart mourning the Guardians, her faith in Aria a spark of hope.

Abilities

Health: 70 | Stamina: 50 | Essence: 20 | Agility: 8 | Resistance: 5

Flute Pulse: Sonic wave (10 damage, 10 Stamina)—stuns with haunting notes.

Wind Lash: Gust strike (8 damage, 8 Stamina)—pushes foes back.

Wind Whisper: Boosts puzzle-solving (+5% speed)—guides with wind’s song.

Background

Born an acolyte, Liora lost her family to raptors at 18, inheriting the Wind Flute from her mother, Veyra. She’s guarded the Temple alone since, striking its chimes against shadow—Aria’s fiery hair rekindles her purpose as Elyra’s echo.

Role

Liora mentors Aria in the Temple—her Echo Chime and wind puzzles shape her trials, her stern wisdom pushing Aria beyond doubt, a keeper of the song honoring Elyra’s legacy.
