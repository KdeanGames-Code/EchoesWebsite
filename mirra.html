Mirra

Elder of hope’s fragile thread

The Tribal Elder

Profile

Name: Mirra

Role: Ally, Elder of the Tribal Enclave

Gender: Female

Age: 67

Appearance

Frail and bent—thin frame beneath a reed-woven shawl, trembling hands clutching a glowing Reed Totem. Wispy white hair threaded with reeds rustles faintly, gray eyes gleam with soft defiance, a faded crown enduring shadow’s bite.

Personality

Gentle and resolute—Mirra’s ash-soft voice carries unyielding hope, her compassion a quiet flame. Wise yet fragile, she sees Aria as salvation, her frailty masking a heart that fights for the Enclave’s light.

Abilities

Health: 50 | Stamina: 40 | Essence: 15 | Agility: 6 | Resistance: 2

Totem Pulse: Healing wave (+10 Health)—a gentle glow aids allies.

Hope’s Whisper: Boosts morale (+5 Resilience)—her voice strengthens Aria.

Background

Born a healer’s daughter in the Enclave, Mirra rose as elder after losing kin to gruntlings and Kael’s betrayal. She’s resisted shadow’s tide, her Stormforged Spear a gift to Aria—her fiery hair a spark of hope to save her people.

Role

Mirra anchors Aria’s Enclave choice—her plea shapes mercy or ruin, her faith softening Aria’s guilt, a frail light guiding her through shadow’s test.
